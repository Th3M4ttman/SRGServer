About me



Why did you make this?


I'm a BiSexual BiRomantic Cis Male and as such am interested in the complexity of Sexuality Romance and Gender. The saying "Gender isn't binary" sort of irked me as a programmer. By definition Binary can mean "relating to, composed of, or involving two things." but it can also mean the numbering system of base 2. With that thought i wondered: What would be an efficient way to represent gender in base 2. That thought snowballed and i looked up and i had created a method to show Gender Romantic and Sexual orientation in 1 single byte.


I'm not 100% whether this encapsulates all forms of Sexuality/Romance/Gender but this is a work in progress and i will update it when im made aware of its inadequacy.
